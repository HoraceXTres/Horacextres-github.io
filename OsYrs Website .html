Who We Are

Self Sufficiency
is a Practice

My name is Horace X. I built OSYRS — Operation Save YourSelf — around one simple belief: that every person has the ability to feed themselves, take care of their health, and live with less dependence on systems that were never designed with them in mind.

"The sun is ancient. It has been here long before us and will be here long after. Your job is to understand it and use it to your advantage."

I come to your space, assess your setup, and build you something that actually grows. From custom cedar raised beds to herb starter plants — everything I do is about getting you one seed closer to self sufficiency.
